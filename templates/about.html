Features we cover:

Remove Punctuations TextUtils can help Remove punctuations from the given text. The pucttions are a saved dictionary so anything saved in text matching punctuations is removed.

UpperCase All TextUtils also offer basic conversion of text into uppercase.

Remove New Line TextUtils also offer a feature to remove new lines from your text and bring multiple paragraphs into a single paragraph to mahe your wotk easier.

Extra Space Remover Extra spaces are done oftenly by mistakes so we offer you a way so that your text can be free from these small mistakes.
